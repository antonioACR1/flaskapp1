Suggest me a song now!

It's simple!

By answering 10 simple personality questions, we will suggest 3 songs that you might like.

How does it work?

We store the preferred songs of thousands of people as well as their personality answers. Using this dataset, we apply a machine learning algorithm to your answers. Then you receive three songs.

After this, we will show three YouTube links corresponding to the suggested songs. If the song is not on YouTube yet, we will show the artist and name.

Aren't you happy with our suggestions? Please help us to improve!

If you don't like any of the suggested songs, you can tell us your 3 preferred songs! If you do this, then other people with a similar personality might enjoy your 3 preferred songs!
